MenuCaptain
Remember every great meal.

MenuCaptain is a private, AI-powered notebook for eating out. It remembers the restaurants you visit, the dishes you loved, and the ones you'd skip - so the next time you sit down somewhere, you already know what's good.

Scan any menu
Point your camera at a physical menu and MenuCaptain turns it into a clean digital one - sections, dishes, descriptions, prices.
Remember what you loved
Rate every dish "Again" or "Skip" as you eat. Next visit, your favorites are waiting at the top.
Ask the menu a question
"Something light?" "Best value?" "What did I love here?" MenuCaptain answers from that restaurant's actual menu - and from your own history.
Scan the check
Snap the receipt when the bill comes - MenuCaptain reads it, asks which were yours, and logs them with their prices.
Calories, if you're curious
Snap a photo of your plate for an estimate of calories and macros. It's a number for memory, never a budget - MenuCaptain doesn't scold.
Find them on a map
See every place you've logged as pins on a map, favorites in the accent color - your spots at a glance.
Your year in food
A recap of where you ate, what you discovered, and the dish you couldn't stop ordering - plus a photo passport of every meal.
Share the love
Turn a favorite place into a picture card to text a friend, or publish a list of your best spots in any city - no app needed on their end.

Not a diet app. Not a review site. Your own private record - no followers, no guilt. Just you, remembering and enjoying what you eat.
